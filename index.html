About

Dodson Harp Studio provides elegant harp music for weddings, ceremonies, and special events. Megan Dodson is a professional harpist with experience performing both classical and contemporary repertoire.

Services

Live harp music is available for weddings, receptions, private events, and other special occasions. Both pedal harp and Celtic harp are offered, depending on the setting and musical needs.

Music

Repertoire includes classical selections, contemporary music, sacred pieces, and Celtic favorites. A full music list is available upon request.

Audio samples can be found on Facebook under Megan Dodson, Harpist/Pianist.

For availability, pricing, or additional information, please get in touch to discuss your event and musical needs.
